## Traceability Completeness

Review the following to confirm traceability completeness before approval:

- [ ] Every user need (UN-xxx) is traced to at least one design input (DI-xxx).
- [ ] Every design input (DI-xxx) is traced to at least one design output (DO-xxx).
- [ ] Every safety-related design input is traced to a risk control.
- [ ] Every design output is traced to at least one verification record.
- [ ] Every user need is traced to at least one validation record.
- [ ] All risk controls are verified.
- [ ] All gaps in traceability are identified and addressed.
